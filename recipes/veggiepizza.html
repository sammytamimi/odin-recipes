Veggie Pizza


Description

Indulge in the refreshing flavors of a delectable Veggie Ranch Crescent Pizza. Crafted with care, this delightful dish starts with a golden-brown crescent roll crust, creating the perfect canvas. A velvety blend of sour cream, cream cheese, and zesty ranch seasoning is then spread atop the crust, offering a smooth and tangy foundation. Vibrant veggies, including chopped fresh broccoli, thinly-sliced radishes, finely-chopped onion, bell pepper, grated carrot, and sliced celery, are artfully arranged over the creamy layer, adding layers of color and crispness. After a brief chill, the pizza is cut into 16 delectable squares, presenting a balanced medley of flavors and textures that's perfect for sharing or savoring solo. The Veggie Ranch Crescent Pizza is a delightful harmony of freshness and creaminess, making it an ideal treat for gatherings or a satisfying snack for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 (8 ounce) packages refrigerated crescent rolls
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1 (1 ounce) package ranch seasoning mix
  • 1 teaspoon dried dill weed
  • ¼ teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1 ½ cups chopped fresh broccoli
  • ½ cup halved and thinly-sliced radishes
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 1 stalk celery, thinly sliced

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Spray a jelly roll pan with nonstick cooking spray.
  2. Press crescent roll dough into the prepared jelly roll pan to form a crust. Let stand 5 minutes. Pierce with a fork.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until dough is fully cooked and golden brown, about 10 minutes. Let cool completely.
  4. Combine sour cream, cream cheese, ranch seasoning mix, dill, and garlic salt in a medium mixing bowl. Spread the cream cheese mixture on top of cooled crust.
  5. Arrange broccoli, radish, onion, bell pepper, carrot, and celery on top of the cream cheese mixture.
  6. Cover and let chill, 1 to 2 hours. Cut chilled pizza into 16 squares to serve.
