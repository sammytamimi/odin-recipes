Ramen Noodle Soup

Description

Experience the comforting delight of Quick and Easy Ramen. With a base of 3 ½ cups of flavorful vegetable broth, this dish offers a warm embrace on chilly days. The addition of a 3.5-ounce package of ramen noodles, generously sprinkled with dried vegetables, infuses the broth with a delightful taste and texture. Enhancing the flavor profile, 2 teaspoons of soy sauce and a hint of minced fresh ginger root provide a subtle yet aromatic punch. A touch of chili oil lends a gentle heat that awakens the senses, while a teaspoon of sesame oil adds an alluring depth. As the noodles soften and flavors meld, this savory concoction simmers to perfection in just 10 minutes. Upon serving, a sprinkling of sliced green onions offers a vibrant finish to this effortlessly satisfying dish

Ingredients

  • 3 ½ cups vegetable broth
  • 1 (3.5 ounce) package ramen noodles with dried vegetables
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce
  • ½ teaspoon chili oil
  • ½ teaspoon minced fresh ginger root
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 green onions, sliced

Steps

  1. In a medium saucepan combine broth and noodles.
  2. Cover and bring to a boil over high heat; stir to break up noodles.
  3. Reduce heat to medium and add soy sauce, chili oil, and ginger.
  4. Simmer, uncovered, for 10 minutes.
  5. Stir in sesame oil and garnish with green onions.
